Services

C.Geddes Design & Finishing offers a wide range of high-quality construction services tailored to meet the needs of our clients. Our skilled workforce is dedicated to delivering excellence in every project we undertake.

General Construction

We offer comprehensive construction services, handling projects of any size and complexity with our skilled in-house workforce. Our expertise includes selective demolition, site work, concrete, masonry, and carpentry.

Pre-construction Planning

Our pre-construction services focus on thorough planning, ensuring all potential conflicts are identified and resolved early. We work closely with clients to provide practical and cost-effective solutions.

Construction Management

Our management team is actively involved in every project, ensuring that all work meets our high standards of quality and craftsmanship. We oversee every aspect from management to field supervision and actual construction.

Quality Craftsmanship

We pride ourselves on our reputation for delivering high-quality work. Our team is committed to maintaining this standard in every project, ensuring client satisfaction and building long-lasting relationships.

About Us

C.Geddes Design & Finishing is a family-owned and operated general contracting firm with over 15 years of experience in the construction industry. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and client satisfaction has been the foundation of our business since the very beginning. We take pride in being involved in every aspect of our projects, from initial planning to final construction, ensuring that our clients receive the highest level of service and expertise.
